About company

The company "TPK" TRIUMPH "LLC, has been working for more than a year on the market of deliveries of limestone quarries, for this time has already established itself as a reliable and responsible supplier of quality goods.


The main activity of the company is the supply of limestone for sugar and metallurgy industry, which meets all State standards and technological characteristics that allow to produce a quality final product.


Our products are made only from the best Podolsky limestone, which has some of the highest consumer characteristics on the territory of modern Ukraine. Limestone mining in Podillia began more At the end of the XIX century and to this day is one of the main directions in the mining industry Podillia.


The purpose of the enterprise's activities will appear - the satisfaction of the Client's needs with the end product at the highest level.


If you are interested in the products of our company, our specialists will gladly advise you on all matters relating to the products at any time convenient for you. With basic information and goods, you can be found here.
